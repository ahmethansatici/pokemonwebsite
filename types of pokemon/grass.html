Grass

Many Grass types are based on plants and fungi, not necessarily grass. Many Grass-type Pokémon also belong to the Plant Egg Group. Several Grass types are paired with the Poison type, reflecting the toxicity of several plants towards mainly humans. Most Grass-types are also simply animals with plant life attached to them. Some Grass-types are also based on mythical creatures.
